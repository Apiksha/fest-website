About Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University

Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University was established under an Act of J&K State Legislature in 1999 as a fully residential and technical university, the first of its kind in J&K. Recognized by UGC under Section 2(f) & 12(B) of the UGC Act of 1956, the university receives funding from UGC.

The university ranked 31st among Architecture Institutions, 151-200 among Engineering Institutions, and 51-100 amongst top Universities in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF 2024) declared by the MHRD, Govt. of India.
