ICMRSF-2025

"Vasudha"

Two-day International Conference on
Multidisciplinary Research on Sustainable Future

The Annual Conference, ICMRSF-2025, organized by the Board of Professional Activities, Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University (SMVDU), is a key event within the University Annual Festival Ekatva '25. This two-day international conference (Feb 28 – Mar 1, 2025) serves as a platform for scholars, researchers, and professionals across disciplines—sciences, engineering, literature, business, and more—to engage in critical discussions on sustainability.

Focusing on "Multidisciplinary Research for a Sustainable Future," the conference addresses urgent global challenges such as climate change, resource depletion, and social inequities. By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange, SMVDU invites participants to contribute research abstracts and be part of this vital discourse on sustainable development.
